Valve Reevaluating Steam Frame Release Timeline and Pricing

Valve announces delays for Steam Frame and Steam Machine due to global memory and storage shortages. Learn about the revised timeline and pricing challenges.

Valve has announced that it needs to "revisit" the official shipping schedule and pricing for its upcoming Steam Frame and Steam Machine. This shift is primarily due to the ongoing global shortages in memory and storage components.

Initial Launch Plans

When these devices were first revealed last November, Valve initially targeted an "early 2026" launch. At the time, the company informed UploadVR that it aimed to price the Steam Frame below the $1,000 Index kit, while the Steam Machine was expected to offer value comparable to a custom-built PC.

Industry-Wide Challenges

However, in a recent Steam blog update, Valve explained that the "limited availability and rising costs" of essential hardware parts have forced a strategy change.

Official Statement from Valve

"At the time of our November announcement, we expected to provide specific pricing and launch windows by now. However, the industry-wide shortage of memory and storage has intensified. These constraints mean we must re-evaluate our exact shipping timelines and price points, particularly for the Steam Machine and Steam Frame."

Current Status and Timeline

Despite these challenges, Valve's objective remains to release both products within the first half of 2026. The company noted it is still working to finalize "concrete pricing and launch dates" that account for the volatile market conditions.

What This Means

Valve concluded the update by promising to keep the community informed as they solidify their plans. The company remains committed to delivering both the Steam Frame and Steam Machine, but wants to ensure pricing remains competitive despite supply chain pressures.

Looking Forward

While the delays are disappointing for eager gamers, Valve's transparency about the challenges shows their commitment to delivering quality products at fair prices. The gaming community will be watching closely as the company navigates these industry-wide supply constraints.
